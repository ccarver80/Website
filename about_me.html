About Me

My name is Chris Carver and I live in North Dakota. I am a computer nerd at heart, I grew up in the 90's with a 1998 computer. I was always fascinated with how it worked inside and out so much that once we upgraded our computer to a newer Dell in the 2000's, I remember tearing apart the old Gateway and inspecting all the components.

I've always been the computer guru of the family, when someone has a problem and they come to me I usually dive right into the problem and try my hardest to figure out what is going on. More often than not I teach myself something new in the process.

I grew up in a oilfield family, I have been working in the oilfield my whole life. Deciding that I don't have any other skills, I sat down one day and started teaching myself how to program and code. I started with Python, as research told me it was one of the easiest languages and one of the most popular. I branched off that a bit and started learning web development as well. I started to build this website as I was learning. There were multiple times that I got frustrated and deleted all the HTML and CSS and started over.
